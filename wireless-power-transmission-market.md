<p><strong>The Wireless Power Transmission Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Near-Field Technology</li><li>Far-Field Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless power transmission market is divided into two main types: near-field and far-field technology. Near-field technology operates at short distances, typically using inductive coupling or resonant inductive coupling, ideal for charging devices like smartphones or electric vehicles. It relies on magnetic fields to transfer energy efficiently over small gaps. In contrast, far-field technology enables energy transmission over longer distances, utilizing microwave or laser beams, making it suitable for applications like powering remote sensors or drones. Both types cater to distinct applications and market needs.</p></p>
