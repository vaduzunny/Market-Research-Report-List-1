<p><p>The pneumatic caster market comprises two primary types: rigid casters and swivel casters. Rigid casters are fixed in one direction, providing stability and supporting heavy loads, making them ideal for straight-line movement. Conversely, swivel casters offer enhanced maneuverability, allowing for easier navigation in tight spaces as they can rotate 360 degrees. Both types can be equipped with pneumatic wheels, which provide shock absorption and improved traction, making them suitable for various applications in warehouses, factories, and transport vehicles.</p></p>
