<p><p>Fertigation and chemigation are advanced agricultural practices that integrate irrigation systems with the application of fertilizers and chemicals. Fertigation involves delivering nutrients through irrigation systems directly to crops, enhancing nutrient uptake and efficiency. Chemigation, on the other hand, refers to the application of chemicals, such as pesticides and herbicides, through irrigation systems, optimizing pest control and reducing labor costs.</p><p>The Fertigation & Chemigation Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rising food demand, the need for sustainable agricultural practices, and increasing adoption of precision farming techniques. As water scarcity continues to challenge traditional farming methods, these technologies offer efficient solutions by minimizing water use and maximizing crop yield.
