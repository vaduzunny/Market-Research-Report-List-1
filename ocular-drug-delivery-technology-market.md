<p><strong>The Ocular Drug Delivery Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Topical</li><li>Ocular Inserts</li><li>Iontophoresis</li><li>Intraocular Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ocular drug delivery technology market encompasses various methods for administering therapeutic agents to the eye. Topical delivery involves eye drops or gels, providing ease of use but often limited absorption. Ocular inserts are designed for sustained release of medication, enhancing efficacy. Iontophoresis utilizes electrical currents to improve drug penetration into ocular tissues. Intraocular implants provide a long-term solution by delivering medication directly within the eye, allowing for continuous treatment and better management of ocular diseases.</p></p>
