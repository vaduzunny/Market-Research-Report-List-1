<p><p>Ductless heat pumps are categorized into single-zone and multi-zone systems. Single-zone units condition a specific space, ideal for smaller areas or individual rooms, providing efficient heating and cooling with minimal installation effort. Multi-zone systems, on the other hand, serve multiple spaces using one outdoor unit connected to multiple indoor units, allowing for tailored temperature control and increased energy efficiency in larger homes or commercial buildings. Both types offer flexibility and are increasingly popular for their versatility and lower energy consumption.</p></p>
