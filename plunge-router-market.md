<p><p>A plunge router is a versatile woodworking tool designed for precision cutting and shaping of materials, including wood, plastic, and composite materials. This tool is widely utilized in cabinetry, furniture making, and DIY projects, making it a staple in both professional and amateur workshops.</p><p>The plunge router market is experiencing significant growth, driven by rising demand for woodworking tools and increasing interest in DIY home improvement projects.
