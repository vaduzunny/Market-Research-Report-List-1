<p><p>Portable beach umbrellas are designed to provide shade and protection from the sun, with two main types: center pole umbrellas and offset pole umbrellas. Center pole umbrellas feature a single pole in the middle, offering a traditional design and stability but limited shading options for larger areas. In contrast, offset pole umbrellas have a side-mounted pole that allows for versatile positioning and greater coverage, making them ideal for accommodating furniture or multiple beach-goers while maximizing shade.</p></p>
