<p><strong>The UV Blocking Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Clear UV Blocking Film</li><li>Dyed UV Blocking Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV blocking film market comprises two main types: clear UV blocking film and dyed UV blocking film. Clear UV blocking film is designed to allow visible light to pass through while effectively blocking harmful ultraviolet rays, making it ideal for windows and displays without altering the aesthetic. In contrast, dyed UV blocking film incorporates pigments that absorb UV radiation, offering both protection and color. These films are commonly used in automotive, architectural, and consumer products to enhance UV protection and reduce fading.</p></p>
