<p><p>The air purification machine market can be categorized into three main types: passive, active, and active-passive hybrid systems. Passive air purifiers rely on natural air flow and filtration materials to capture pollutants, often using HEPA filters. Active systems utilize technology, such as ionization or UV light, to actively eliminate airborne contaminants. Active-passive hybrid models combine both methods, enhancing efficiency by integrating filtration with active purification technologies, thereby offering comprehensive solutions for improving indoor air quality.</p></p>
