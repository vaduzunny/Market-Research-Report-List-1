<p><strong>The Waterjet Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Intensifier Pump</li><li>Rotary Direct Drive Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterjet pump market features primarily two types: linear intensifier pumps and rotary direct drive pumps. Linear intensifier pumps utilize a piston mechanism to increase water pressure efficiently, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, rotary direct drive pumps employ an electric motor to directly drive the pump, offering simplified operation and maintenance. Both types serve diverse industries, including manufacturing and construction, providing precise cutting solutions and offering advantages in efficiency, cost, and energy consumption.</p></p>
