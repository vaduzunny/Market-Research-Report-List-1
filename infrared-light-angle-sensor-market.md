<p><strong>The Infrared Light Angle Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave IR (SWIR)</li><li>Mid Wave IR (MWIR)</li><li>Long Wave (LWIR)</li><li>Far Wave (FWIR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared light angle sensor market is segmented into four main types based on wavelength: Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid Wave Infrared (MWIR), Long Wave Infrared (LWIR), and Far Wave Infrared (FWIR). SWIR sensors operate in the 0.9 to 1.7 micrometer range and are ideal for applications like surveillance and agricultural monitoring. MWIR sensors, in the 3 to 5 micrometer range, are used in thermal imaging. LWIR sensors, from 8 to 12 micrometers, excel in temperature sensing, while FWIR covers wavelengths beyond 12 micrometers, often used in environmental monitoring.</p></p>
