<p><p>The PV Array Combiner Box market is categorized into two main types: DC Smart PV Combiner Boxes and AC Smart PV Combiner Boxes. DC Smart PV Combiner Boxes aggregate the output from multiple photovoltaic (PV) strings into one DC circuit, often incorporating features like surge protection and monitoring capabilities. In contrast, AC Smart PV Combiner Boxes are designed to combine the output from inverters, converting DC to AC power while providing enhanced efficiency and grid compatibility, as well as advanced monitoring and safety functions.</p></p>
