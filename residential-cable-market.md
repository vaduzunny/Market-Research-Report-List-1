<p><p>The residential cable market is divided into low voltage and high voltage cable types. Low voltage cables are designed for safe distribution of electricity in homes, typically used for lighting, heating, and electrical appliances. Meanwhile, high voltage cables are employed for transmitting electricity over long distances, connecting substations and power plants to residential areas. Both cable types are crucial for ensuring reliable energy supply, with low voltage cables focusing on internal distribution and high voltage cables managing external transmission networks.</p></p>
