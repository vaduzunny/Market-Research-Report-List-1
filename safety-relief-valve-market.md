<p><p>The safety relief valve market includes various types, primarily focusing on spring-type and lever-type relief valves. Spring-type relief valves operate using a spring mechanism to control pressure and release excess fluid, making them suitable for various applications. Lever-type relief valves utilize a lever arm to amplify the force, allowing for precise control in managing pressure levels. Both valve types are crucial in ensuring system safety by preventing overpressure situations in industrial, petrochemical, and other critical processes.</p></p>
