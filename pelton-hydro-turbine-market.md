<p><p>The Pelton Hydro Turbine is a type of impulse turbine designed for high-head hydroelectric power generation. It operates by utilizing the kinetic energy of water jets directed at spoon-shaped buckets mounted on the turbine wheel, allowing it to extract energy efficiently from water at high elevations. This design makes it particularly suitable for applications in mountainous regions and where water sources have significant height differences.</p><p>The Pelton Hydro Turbine Market is projected to experience substantial growth, with an anticipated compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 10.9% during the forecast period.
