<p><strong>The Gastrointestinal Electrocardiograph Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrogastroenterography (EGEG)</li><li>Cutaneous Electrogastrography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gastrointestinal electrocardiograph market encompasses two main types: Electrogastroenterography (EGEG) and Cutaneous Electrogastrography. EGEG is a diagnostic technique that records gastric electrical activity via electrodes placed on the skin, offering insights into gastrointestinal motility disorders. Cutaneous Electrogastrography involves surface electrodes to measure gastric myoelectrical signals non-invasively, aiding in the assessment of digestive conditions like dyspepsia. Both methods facilitate the understanding of gastrointestinal health and function, enhancing diagnosis and treatment strategies for related disorders.</p></p>
