<p><p>The absorbable adhesion barrier market comprises three primary formulation types: film, gel, and liquid. Film formulations provide a solid, thin layer that mechanically separates tissues, reducing adhesion risk. Gel formulations offer a viscous consistency that adheres to surfaces, facilitating tissue healing while minimizing scar formation. Liquid formulations allow for easy application and shape conformity, delivering a protective barrier during surgical procedures. Each type serves the purpose of preventing postoperative adhesions, enhancing recovery outcomes and patient safety.</p></p>
