<p><p>Terminal POS systems are classified into two main market types: fixed and mobile. Fixed type POS systems are stationary setups, often found in retail environments, providing robustness and extensive functionalities for processing transactions. They typically include hardware like cash registers, barcode scanners, and receipt printers. In contrast, mobile type POS systems operate on handheld devices or tablets, allowing for transactions anywhere, enhancing customer service and flexibility. Mobile systems are ideal for businesses requiring mobility, such as food trucks and pop-up shops.</p></p>
