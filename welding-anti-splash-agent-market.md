<p><p>The welding anti-splash agent market comprises four main types: paste, mushy, gelatinous, and water liquid. Paste agents offer a thick consistency for enhanced adhesion and protection against sparks. Mushy agents provide a semi-solid texture, balancing flowability and coverage. Gelatinous types exhibit a flexible, jelly-like consistency, ideal for intricate welds. Water liquid agents, being the most fluid, effectively spread over surfaces, minimizing splatter while cooling the weld area. Each type serves to improve efficiency and safety in welding processes.</p></p>
