<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Couplers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Mode Couplers</li><li>Multi-mode Couplers</li><li>Polarization Maintaining (PM) Couplers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber optic couplers market consists of three main types: Single Mode Couplers, which are designed for use with single mode fibers, allowing for long-distance signal transmission with minimal loss; Multi-mode Couplers, suited for multi-mode fibers, facilitating higher bandwidths over shorter distances; and Polarization Maintaining (PM) Couplers, which preserve the polarization state of light for specialized applications. Each type serves distinct purposes in telecommunications, data centers, and industrial applications, playing a crucial role in ensuring efficient fiber optic communication.</p></p>
