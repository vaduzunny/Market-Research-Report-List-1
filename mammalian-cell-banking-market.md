<p><p>The Mammalian Cell Banking market comprises various types of cell banks essential for biopharmaceutical production. Master Cell Banks (MCBs) are the primary source of cell lines, maintaining genetic integrity. Working Cell Banks (WCBs) are derived from MCBs, used for routine production. End of Production Cell Banks (EOPCBs) store cells post-production for future use. Research & Development Cell Banks support experimental studies and process development. Other cell banks may include specialized or customized cell lines for specific applications, enhancing research versatility.</p></p>
