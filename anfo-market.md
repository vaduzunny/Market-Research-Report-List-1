<p><ul><li>Powder Type</li><li>Granular Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ANFO, or Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil, is categorized into two main market types: Powder Type and Granular Type. Powder Type ANFO features a fine, mixed composition, enhancing detonation efficiency in confined blasts. It is ideal for applications requiring precise control and quick initiation. Granular Type ANFO consists of larger particles, providing enhanced water resistance and stability in various environmental conditions. This type is commonly used in mining and construction, where larger volumes and prolonged storage are necessary. Each type serves specific needs within the explosive market.</p></p>
