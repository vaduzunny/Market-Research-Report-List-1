<p><strong>The Physisorption Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Area Analyzer</li><li>Pore Size Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Physisorption analyzers measure the adsorption of gas molecules on solid surfaces, distinguishing primarily between surface area analyzers and pore size analyzers. Surface area analyzers determine the total surface area of a material by evaluating the amount of gas adsorbed at varying pressure points, providing insights into porosity and material texture. Pore size analyzers, on the other hand, focus on characterizing the dimensions and distribution of pores within a material, crucial for applications in catalysis, filtration, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
