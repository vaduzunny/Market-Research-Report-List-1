<p><p>The packaging robots market is categorized into primary and secondary packaging. Primary packaging robots handle the initial packaging of products, such as filling, sealing, and labeling individual items, ensuring they are ready for consumer sale. Secondary packaging robots, on the other hand, focus on grouping or bundling primary packages, organizing them into cartons or pallets for shipping. Both types of robots enhance efficiency, reduce labor costs, and improve accuracy in packaging processes across various industries.</p></p>
