<p><p>Building-Integrated Photovoltaic (BIPV) skylights primarily use two types of solar panel technologies: crystalline panels and thin film panels. Crystalline panels, known for their high efficiency and durability, are typically made from silicon and are favored for their performance in various lighting conditions. In contrast, thin film panels utilize a variety of materials and are lighter and more flexible, making them easier to integrate into architectural designs. Both types contribute to energy generation while enhancing the aesthetic appeal of buildings.</p></p>
