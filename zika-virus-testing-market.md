<p><p>The Zika Virus Testing Market is primarily divided into two types: molecular tests and serologic tests. Molecular tests, such as PCR, detect the virus's genetic material in blood or other fluids, providing early diagnosis during an active infection. In contrast, serologic tests identify antibodies produced in response to the virus, indicating past infection or exposure. Both testing types play crucial roles in diagnosing Zika virus infections, guiding public health responses, and monitoring potential outbreaks.</p></p>
