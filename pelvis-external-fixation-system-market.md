<p><ul><li>Tubular</li><li>Circular</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pelvis external fixation system market comprises two primary types: tubular and circular. Tubular systems utilize straight rods and bolts, providing stability for pelvic fractures and facilitating easy application. They are often preferred for straightforward cases. In contrast, circular systems consist of rings connected by rods, allowing for greater adaptability and support in complex fractures. These systems enable precise adjustments and are particularly useful in reconstructive surgeries, thus meeting diverse clinical needs in pelvic fracture management.</p></p>
