<p><strong>The Oral Antidiabetic (OAD) Treatment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sulphonylureas</li><li>Biguanides</li><li>Intestinal α-Glucosidase Inhibitors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oral antidiabetic (OAD) treatment market encompasses several categories. Sulphonylureas stimulate insulin secretion from the pancreas, effectively lowering blood sugar levels. Biguanides, primarily metformin, enhance insulin sensitivity and reduce glucose production in the liver. Intestinal α-glucosidase inhibitors slow carbohydrate absorption in the intestines, helping to manage postprandial blood glucose levels. Other OADs include DPP-4 inhibitors and SGLT2 inhibitors, which employ different mechanisms to control blood sugar. Together, these types provide diverse treatment options for managing diabetes.</p></p>
