<p><ul><li>UV</li><li>Non-UV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tape for wafer dicing market is divided into UV and non-UV types. UV tapes utilize ultraviolet light for curing, offering precise adhesion and clean removal, making them ideal for applications where minimal residue is critical. Non-UV tapes, on the other hand, are designed for standard adhesion without the need for light activation, catering to general dicing requirements. Both types serve to protect wafers during the cutting process, ensuring operational efficiency and quality in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
