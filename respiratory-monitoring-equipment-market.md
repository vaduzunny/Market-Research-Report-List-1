<p><p>The respiratory monitoring equipment market includes various types essential for assessing lung function and oxygen levels. Pulse oximeters measure blood oxygen saturation non-invasively. Peak flow meters assess airway obstruction, aiding asthma management. Carbon dioxide analyzers monitor CO2 levels in respiratory gases. Spirometers evaluate lung capacity and airflow. Polysomnography records sleep patterns for diagnosing sleep disorders. Gas analyzers assess respiratory gases for medical purposes. Other equipment may include pulse monitors and respiratory rate monitors, enhancing patient care and diagnostics.</p></p>
