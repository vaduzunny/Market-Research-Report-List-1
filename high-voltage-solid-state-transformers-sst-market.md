<p><p>High Voltage Solid State Transformers (SST) are advanced devices that replace traditional transformers with more efficient and flexible alternatives. The market is categorized into three types based on design complexity and functionality. </p><p>Single-stage SSTs directly convert high voltage to low voltage with minimal components. Two-stage SSTs include an intermediate conversion phase, enhancing performance and stability. Three-stage SSTs offer the highest versatility, enabling multiple voltage and frequency outputs, making them suitable for diverse applications in renewable energy, electric vehicles, and smart grids.</p></p>
