<p><p>The industrial magnetrons market is categorized into two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) and Pulsed. Continuous Wave magnetrons provide a steady microwave output, making them ideal for applications such as industrial heating, drying, and processing. Pulsed magnetrons, on the other hand, emit short bursts of microwaves, which are useful in applications like materials processing and radar. Each type serves distinct industries, with CW being preferred for consistent processes and pulsed for applications needing high peak power.</p></p>
