<p><p>A Self-priming Magnetic Pump is a type of pump that utilizes magnetic coupling to drive the impeller while eliminating the need for mechanical seals. This design allows the pump to operate without needing manual priming, making it efficient and reliable for handling various fluids, including corrosive and highly viscous liquids.
