<p><p>The hydraulic breaker market is primarily divided into two types: handheld hydraulic breakers and machine-mounted hydraulic breakers. Handheld hydraulic breakers are portable tools used for breaking concrete, asphalt, and other materials, making them ideal for small-scale demolition and construction tasks. In contrast, machine-mounted hydraulic breakers are attached to larger construction equipment, such as excavators or backhoes, allowing for heavy-duty demolition and extensive workload management on larger construction sites. Both types enhance efficiency and productivity in construction and demolition projects.</p></p>
