Micro inverters are installed on individual panels, enabling maximum energy harvest and performance monitoring. Other inverter types may include power optimizers and hybrid inverters, which combine features for added flexibility. Each type caters to different installation needs, system sizes, and efficiency goals within residential solar applications.</p></p>
